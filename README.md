### 🗿 The Sculpture Theory

> Every great design is a **discovery**, not an invention.
> The optimal form was already there — hiding inside the problem.
> You chip away until it shows up.

The simplest form almost always wins. Not the cleverest. Not the most "powerful." Just the one that fits.

**The agent types now. You still do the seeing.**
